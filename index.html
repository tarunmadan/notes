Aviation Industry

Timeline

  • 17-Dec-1903 - First successful powered, controlled and sustained airplane flight by Wright Brothers - Orville Wright and Wilber Wright. Plane name was the "Wright Flyer". 12 hp (9 kW) engine. Plane was wrecked and never flown again in the 4th flight.
  • 14-Oct-1904 - First circular flight by "Wright Flyer II". 15 hp (11.2 kW) engine. Complted 105 flights in 1904, longest flight o 5 minutes and 8 seconds.
  • 23-Jun-1905 - First flight of "Wright Flyer III".
  • 22-May-1906 - Patent of flying machine granted to the Wright Brothers.
  • 14-May-1908 - Wright Brothers flew their first passenger, Charles Furnas, one of their mechanics
  • 08-Feb-1908 - Wright Brothers awarded a military contract to build a flying machine.
  • 17-Sep-1908 - Army lieutenant Thomas Selfridge became first airplane crash fatality.
  • 22-Nov-1909 - Wright Company incorporated.
  • 07-Nov-1910 - First known commercial air transport of cargo
